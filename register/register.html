LPU INSTANT CONNECT - Registration Page

LPU Instant Connect is a powerful extension designed to streamline the process of connecting to LPU Wifi Networks

With the help of this extension we intend to eliminate the hassle of manually entering your passwords and credentials. Your credentials are not shared and are only stored in your browser's local storage.

To Begin, input your Registration Id and Wifi Password in the input box below and click submit.

To change the credentials you can click on the extension icon again. This will open the Change Credentials Page.
